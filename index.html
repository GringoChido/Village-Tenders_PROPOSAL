Section Two

What We're Building
in Phase 1

Eight pieces, woven together so they all share one brand voice and one visual language from day one.

One

Brand, Mission, Vision, and Copy Deck

A document you'll receive in week 1. It includes your refreshed color palette, typography, logo direction, mission statement, vision, brand voice, and the core messages everything flows from.

You sit with it. You approve it. We pause until you do. Nothing else gets built before you say yes to this.

Two

Wireframes for the Website

Before we write any code, you see the structure of every page laid out in clear wireframes. You approve them, or we revise. This is the second small approval gate, and it saves enormous rework later.

Three

Your Custom Website

Custom-coded (not a template, not Squarespace). Stack: Next.js + Tailwind + shadcn/ui + Framer Motion for the gentle animations. Hosted on our Netlify during the build. Editable content via a tiny Sanity CMS so you can update copy yourself without touching code.

The page set: Home, About, The Leadership Course (your catalyst page), Retreats, 1:1 Sessions, Journal (your blog), Contact and Booking (your existing Calendly embedded). Plus a lead capture form on the homepage that adds new subscribers directly into your Flodesk, and Google integration for the assets and calendar feeds we'll need.

Four

Social Templates and a 2-Month Content Plan

15 brand-aligned templates built inside your existing Canva account. Quote cards, retreat reveals, journal post promos, behind-the-scenes, testimonial cards, story highlights, Pinterest pins, LinkedIn graphics. You duplicate, swap copy, post.

Plus a 60-day content calendar mapped to your sales funnel. Every post has a purpose, whether it's driving traffic to the leadership course, the lead magnet, or a journal post. You'll never wake up wondering what to post.

Five

The AI Image System

A custom prompt library tuned to your brand, built around Artlist.io ($300 a year, includes their full stock photo, video, and music library as a bonus). 30+ ready-to-use prompts for the images you'll need most. Plus a short walkthrough video showing you how to use it. You'll generate brand-aligned images in 60 seconds.

Six

Ten Launch Blog Posts

Your blog launches with ten complete posts written in your voice, optimized for SEO and AEO, each with a custom AI-generated brand-aligned hero image, internal links to your offers, pull quotes for sharing, and ready-to-go social captions and email versions. Topics chosen with you to do three things at once: introduce who you are, answer the questions your ideal people are searching for, and gently drive traffic toward your leadership course.

Seven

Full SEO and AEO Foundation

SEO is the work of being found on Google. Page titles, meta descriptions, headings, image alt text, structured data, sitemap, fast loading, mobile-first design. All built in.

AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) is the newer, faster-growing cousin. More and more people are asking ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity for recommendations directly. AEO is the work of making sure those AI tools find you, trust you, and recommend you. We do all of it: the special llms.txt file, schema markup, FAQ structures AI can quote directly, author authority signals, and clear topical depth around your specific niche.

What's a good leadership program for women re-entering work after kids?

Six months after launch, when someone asks an AI for a recommendation in your space, your name is one of the answers.
